How to Create and Join a Video Meeting

Step 1: Open the Meeting Page

Visit the main page of our application. You will see a form asking you to enter a room name. This name will be used to create or join a video chat room.

Step 2: Invite Other People

To invite people, copy the link below and share it via any platform you want. Ask them to paste this link into their browser to join the room.

Note: If a room with the same name exists, you will automatically join that meeting.

Step 3: Share Screen

You can share your screen, and other people can view it in HD quality.

Step 4: Use Meeting Controls

Control buttons at the bottom of the screen allow you to mute/unmute your microphone, enable/disable your camera, or share the room link.

Step 5: Change Background

You can change the background and appear in HD quality to others using this feature.

Troubleshooting Tips

If you experience issues, check the following:

  • Ensure your browser has access to your camera and microphone.
  • Make sure your internet connection is stable.
  • Use an updated version of a supported browser like Google Chrome or Firefox.
